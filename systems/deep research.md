# Deep Research

Deep Research is OpenAI's persistent browsing and research agent system for multi-step information gathering, synthesis, and report generation.

It matters as a long-horizon browsing/research case study. The system is useful for thinking about search planning, source triangulation, multi-hop web navigation, citations, and safety limits for agents that spend many steps on open-ended research.

## Design Pattern

- Let the agent search, read, pivot, and synthesize across many web steps.
- Treat citations and source quality as part of the output contract.
- Evaluate browsing ability separately from general language quality.
