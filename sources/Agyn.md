# Agyn

## Summary

- Autonomous software-engineering agent-team paper that explicitly models development as an organizational process.
- Assigns specialized agents to coordination, research, implementation, and review roles, with isolated sandboxes and structured communication.
- Important because it treats methodology, role separation, communication, and review as first-class sources of agent performance rather than incidental scaffolding.
