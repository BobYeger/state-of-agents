# Learning to Deliberate

## Summary

- Learns meta-actions for deliberation and collaboration rather than relying on a fixed discussion protocol.
- Relevant to multi-agent systems because it treats deliberative state and collaboration policy as trainable.
- Good bridge between planner/executor control structure and multi-agent reinforcement learning.
