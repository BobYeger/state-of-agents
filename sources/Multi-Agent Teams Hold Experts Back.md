# Multi-Agent Teams Hold Experts Back

## Summary

- Corrective paper on self-organizing LLM teams: teams may fail to match their strongest individual member.
- The central failure is not only identifying the expert; it is leveraging expertise without averaging it away through compromise.
- Important counterweight to agent-team enthusiasm because it separates useful organizational structure from unstructured group interaction.
