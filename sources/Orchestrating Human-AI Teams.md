# Orchestrating Human-AI Teams

## Summary

- Research-agenda paper framing the autonomous manager agent as a core challenge for human-AI teams.
- The manager agent decomposes goals, allocates tasks to human and AI workers, monitors progress, adapts, and communicates with stakeholders.
- Important because it shifts agent-team orchestration from all-AI swarms to mixed teams with governance, constraints, and stakeholder communication.
