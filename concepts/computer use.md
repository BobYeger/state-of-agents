# Computer use

Computer-use agents perceive and operate graphical or command-line environments through screenshots, browsers, files, terminals, mouse/keyboard actions, or intermediate action spaces.

This concept connects GUI agents such as [[sources/UI-TARS]] and [[sources/AutoGLM]] with product systems such as [[systems/Operator]], [[systems/Codex]], and [[systems/Claude Code]].
